About Texas Russell Rescue

Texas Russell Rescue is a state-wide network of volunteers dedicated to placing unwanted, displaced or abandoned Jack Russell Terriers into permanent homes. All dogs entering rescue are either temporarily placed in a foster home or must remain with their owners until a suitable home can be found.

Funded by Donations

Our organization is funded entirely by donations. These donations are used for temporary housing, spaying/neutering, veterinarian treatments, food, water, etc.

What Rescue Does
  • Protects the Jack Russell Terrier by educating pet owners and breeders
  • Places Jack Russell Terriers in suitable homes
  • Provides for the temporary health and well being of Jack Russell Terriers in foster care
