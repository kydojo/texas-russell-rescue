About Us

Texas Russell Rescue is the Texas group of Russell Rescue, Inc., a national network of volunteers dedicated to placing unwanted, displaced or abandoned Jack Russell Terriers into permanent homes. We foster these Jack Russells in our homes until we are able to find them their perfect forever home.

Texas Russell Rescue is the Texas group of Russell Rescue, Inc., a national network of volunteers dedicated to placing unwanted, displaced or abandoned Jack Russell Terriers into permanent homes. We foster these Jack Russells in our homes until we are able to find them their perfect forever home.

All dogs entering rescue are either temporarily placed in a foster home or must remain with their owners until a suitable home can be found. Foster space is limited, so foster homes are generally reserved for dogs that are in danger of euthanasia at local shelters or are in an emergency situation. Sometimes, circumstances are such that seeking a new home for your Jack Russell is necessary. If you would like to contact us about surrendering your terrier into our rescue program, please fill out our Owner Surrender Form.

Russell Rescue, Inc. has been approved as a non-profit 501(c)(3) corporation and is funded entirely through donations.
