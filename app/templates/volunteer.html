Volunteer Today

Volunteers are needed in Texas for everything!
  • Transporting dogs
  • Managing social media outlets
  • Posting our adoptable terriers on various websites
  • Conducting home visits for potential adopters
...and so much more!

However, our greatest need is for foster homes.
We can only save as many Jack Russells as we have foster homes for.
Foster homes are the very backbone of our organization. We do not have a shelter or facility, so when we take a Jack Russell into our rescue, we depend on the fosters. Foster provide a temporary home for our Jack Russells until they are adopted. Texas Russell Rescue provides all the necessary veterinary care, medications, food, supplies, etc. for our rescue Jacks. But the foster moms and dads are the ones that provide the love, encouragement, snuggles, belly rubs and a safe place for these little dogs. In return, the dogs provide plenty of licks and tail wags!

If you are willing to open your home to a Jack Russell in need, until a forever home can be found, consider becoming a foster for us. Apply today! Have questions? Contact us and we can try and provide some answers. There are many ways to support us through volunteering.
