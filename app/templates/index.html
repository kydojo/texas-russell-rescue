Spotlight Terrier

Heidi


This pretty and smart girl is Heidi. She is being fostered in Bryan, Texas. Heidi is about 10 months old and is a Jack Russell Terrier mix. She is current on all of her vet work.

As you can see from her video, she is very smart! Her foster mom is a trainer, so Heidi has been trained to follow several commands. She has been very socialized with other dogs as well.

We are looking for the perfect home for this girl. She has been in her foster home since she was a baby. What she really wants is to find her forever family. Heidi will do okay with other dogs and will probably be okay with cats. She would love a playmate in her new home. She prefers a home with no kids. She needs a fenced in yard so she can run and play, and she needs an owner who is not gone all day.

She can be adopted in Texas, Oklahoma or Louisiana. Her adopter must have a good vet reference and must be comfortable with a home visit. Her adoption fee is $200.
